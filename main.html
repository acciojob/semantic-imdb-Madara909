"The Dark Knight" is a 2008 superhero film directed by Christopher Nolan. It follows Batman as he battles the Joker, a criminal mastermind who unleashes chaos in Gotham City. The movie explores themes of morality, sacrifice, and the fine line between heroism and vigilantism.

Actors

The film stars Christian Bale as Bruce Wayne/Batman, Heath Ledger as the Joker, Aaron Eckhart as Harvey Dent, and Gary Oldman as Commissioner Gordon. Heath Ledger's performance as the Joker received critical acclaim and won him a posthumous Academy Award.
